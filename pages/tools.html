What this does

This services is designed to be a two-way conversion to encode and decode:

  • URL Encoded Strings
  • Base64 Encoded Strings
Simply select the type of conversion you want to perform and enter your input. The Copy Result button will automatically copy the result to your clipboard, if your browser supports it, otherwise it will give you an easy way to copy the result from a popup.

You can also drag a file into the input field to convert a file to a Base64 string. All of these conversions happen on your machine, without ever uploading anything to this server!
